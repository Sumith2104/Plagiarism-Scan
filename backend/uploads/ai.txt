Short Essay on Coffee

Coffee is one of the most beloved beverages in the world, enjoyed by millions each morning as a comforting ritual and a source of energy. Made from roasted coffee beans, it carries a rich history that spans centuries and cultures. Whether brewed in a simple kitchen pot or prepared by a skilled barista, coffee has a unique ability to bring people together and spark conversation.

Beyond its pleasant aroma and bold flavor, coffee offers several benefits. It helps improve alertness, enhances concentration, and can even elevate mood. Many students and professionals rely on it to stay focused during long hours of work or study. Coffee shops have also become social hubs where people gather to relax, work, or meet friends.
